About Us

Lister Technologies was established in 2000, headquartered in Chennai. In 2004, we began working as an extended professional services team of a leading e-mail marketing company and started our journey with digital marketing services. Three years later, we began working with a sizeable ed-tech customer and focused on retail and e-commerce, simultaneously building our Salesforce Practice by becoming one of the first partners for Salesforce in India.


By 2009, we started working on the Adobe and Google marketing platforms. That same year, we garnered another feather in our cap when we won a Salesforce Customer Satisfaction Award. In 2010, ten years after we began operations, we opened our second office in Chennai for housing our software development unit. One year later, we added a large cement manufacturing group in our elite list of enterprise clients and a Fortune 500 nutraceutical company, and by 2012, entered into an agreement with Responsys to become their professional services arm for their global clients and to assist them in product engineering.


In the six years, between 2014 and 2020, we grew exponentially, opening a US subsidiary (2016) and partnering with numerous companies there, like BigCommerce, Oracle, WooCommerce, Tealium, Braze, Iterable and Mparticle. We also signed on other notable enterprises and Fortune 500 clients, in hi-tech, BFSI, retail, travel and entertainment verticals.


Today, we are a 300-member company and have established ourselves as one of the top Oracle Marketing Cloud partners, globally, as well as one of the most experienced Salesforce partners in South Asia. We support numerous Fortune 500 companies, delivering immense value to our customers through thought leadership, technical expertise and execution excellence.
